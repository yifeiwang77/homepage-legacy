Yifei Wang 王一飞

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University. I am a member of ZERO Lab and advised by Yisen Wang, Jiansheng Yang, and Zhouchen Lin. I recieved my bachelor's degree also from School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University. I am a recipient of the Best ML Paper Award of ECML-PKDD 2021 and the Silver Best Paper Award of ICML 2021 AML workshop.

I am interested in learning without labels, a.k.a. self-supervised learning (SSL), covering both representation learning p(z|x) and generative modeling p(x). I am devoted to discovering new perspectives for better theoretical understanding and pratical design of SSL algorithms.
